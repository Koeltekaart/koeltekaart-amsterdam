temp_mean = mean surface temperature by neighbourhood obtained from google earth engine
buurt_area = size of neighbourhood as calculated trough data obtained from PDOK
ndvi_mean = mean ndvi in neighbourhood as obtained from ndvi data from google earth engine
water_prc = percentage of surface water in neighbourhood as calculated trough data obtained from PDOK
road_prc = percentage of road in neighbourhood as calculated trough data obtained from PDOK


ndvi = Normalized Difference Vegetation Index, vegitation density as measured by reflected light trough satelites, as photosynthesis absorbs a specific frequency. This number between 0 and 1 indicated the amount of healthy vegitation in an area
